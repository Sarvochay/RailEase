What is RAC?

RAC (Reservation Against Cancellation) is an Indian Railways system where two passengers share one side-lower berth. You get a guaranteed seat to travel, and your ticket upgrades to a full berth when a confirmed passenger cancels.

👥 Pairing Rules (RailEase)

🔑
Same Account / PNR
Same user books multiple RAC tickets → shares their own berth (gender not applied)
👨👩
Different Passengers
Different users paired strictly by gender: males with males, females with females
